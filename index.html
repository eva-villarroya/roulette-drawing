What are trochoids

The curve described by a fixed point as a circle rolls along a straight line.

Get all the details here:

  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Trochoid
  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hypotrochoid
  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Epitrochoid
